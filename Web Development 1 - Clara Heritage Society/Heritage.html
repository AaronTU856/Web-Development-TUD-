About Us



Clara Heritage Society aims to ensure that the rich and valued heritage of

"The Little Town of Clara"

is preserved, rejuvenated, and promoted at every opportunity.









Clara's Heritage


Clara Heritage Society was set up to promote, preserve and enhance the social,
economic, industrial, cultural and natural heritage of Clara town and its environs.
In recent years we participated in National Heritage Week and we are currently working on
a number of exciting initiatives including the enhancement of the Brosna river within the town,
the clearing of St. Brigid's Cemetery Kilbride and
the opening of several new walking trails along the Esker and Clara Bog.
